# Traffic Management

Traffic management ensures the efficient movement of aircraft within the TMA.

---

## Objectives

- Maintain a continuous flow of traffic
- Avoid delays and excessive vectoring
- Balance arrivals and departures

---

## Techniques

- Speed control
- Radar vectoring
- Sequencing

---

## Flow Management

Controllers must:
- Plan ahead
- Anticipate demand
- Adjust early

---

## Common Issues

- Overloading the sequence
- Late corrections
- Poor spacing

---

## Key Principle

> Good traffic management prevents problems rather than reacting to them.
