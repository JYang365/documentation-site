# Responsibilities

The approach controller is responsible for the safe, orderly, and expeditious flow of aircraft within the TMA.

---

## Core Responsibilities

### Separation

- Maintain lateral and vertical separation
- Resolve conflicts early

---

### Sequencing

- Establish an orderly arrival flow
- Deliver stable approaches

---

### Vectoring

- Position aircraft efficiently
- Minimise unnecessary manoeuvring

---

### Communication

- Issue clear and concise instructions
- Monitor pilot compliance

---

## Priority Order

1. Separation  
2. Safety of flight  
3. Efficiency  

---

## Key Principle

> The controller is responsible for all aircraft under their control.
