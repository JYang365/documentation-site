
# 6.7 Missed Approach Handling

A missed approach occurs when an aircraft cannot complete the landing.

---

## Causes

- Unstable approach
- Runway obstruction
- Pilot decision

---

## Controller Responsibilities

- Issue immediate climb instructions
- Ensure separation from other traffic
- Re-sequence aircraft

---

## Phraseology

"Go around, fly runway heading, climb to 3000 feet"

---

## Key Considerations

- Act immediately
- Protect missed approach path
- Maintain situational awareness
