# 6.1 General

Instrument approaches allow aircraft to descend and align with the runway using either ground-based or satellite-based navigation.

Approach control is responsible for:
- Positioning aircraft for approach
- Ensuring correct spacing
- Issuing approach clearance

---

## Key Objectives

- Establish aircraft on final approach
- Maintain separation
- Ensure stable approach profiles

---

## Stabilised Approach Concept

Aircraft should be:
- Correctly aligned with runway
- At appropriate speed
- On a continuous descent profile

Unstable approaches increase the likelihood of go-arounds.
