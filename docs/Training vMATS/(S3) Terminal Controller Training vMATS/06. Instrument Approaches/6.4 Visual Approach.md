
# 6.4 Visual Approach

A visual approach allows the pilot to continue visually to the runway.

---

## Requirements

- Pilot reports runway or traffic in sight
- Weather conditions permit visual reference

---

## Controller Responsibilities

- Ensure separation is maintained
- Issue visual approach clearance
- Monitor aircraft until transfer to tower

---

## Advantages

- Reduces controller workload
- Increases traffic efficiency

---

## Risks

- Loss of situational awareness
- Reduced predictability
