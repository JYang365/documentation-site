
# 6.6 Transfer to Tower

Aircraft shall be transferred to tower once:

- Established on final approach
- Stable and correctly sequenced

---

## Phraseology

"Contact tower 118.1"

---

## Controller Responsibilities

- Ensure spacing is correct
- Ensure no further intervention is required
- Coordinate with tower where necessary

---

## Key Principle

Transfer should be smooth and require no corrective action from tower.
