
# 6.5 Approach Clearance

Approach clearance authorises the aircraft to conduct the approach procedure.

---

## Phraseology

"Cleared ILS approach runway XX"  
"Cleared RNAV approach runway XX"

---

## Requirements Before Clearance

Controllers must ensure:
- Aircraft is correctly positioned
- Separation is achieved
- Aircraft is established or able to establish

---

## Timing

Clearance should be issued:
- Before final intercept where possible
- In sufficient time for pilot preparation
