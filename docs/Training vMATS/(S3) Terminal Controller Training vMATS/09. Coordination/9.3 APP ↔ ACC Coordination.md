# 9.3 APP ↔ ACC Coordination

## Inbound Traffic

ACC transfers aircraft to APP:
- At agreed level
- With appropriate spacing
- On correct routing

---

## APP Responsibilities

- Accept inbound traffic
- Integrate into arrival sequence
- Provide descent and vectoring

---

## Transfer Conditions

Aircraft should be:
- Identified
- Stable
- On correct frequency

---

## Key Principle

APP should receive traffic ready to be sequenced, not corrected.
