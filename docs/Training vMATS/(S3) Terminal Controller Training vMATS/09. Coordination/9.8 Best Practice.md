# 9.8 Best Practice

## Core Principles

- Coordinate early, not late
- Keep communication concise
- Maintain shared situational awareness

---

## Golden Rules

- Never assume the other controller knows
- Confirm critical information
- Plan for missed approaches

---

## Professional Standard

Effective coordination results in:
- Smooth traffic flow
- Minimal intervention required
- Reduced workload across all positions

---

## Key Takeaway

> Good coordination is invisible — poor coordination is obvious.
