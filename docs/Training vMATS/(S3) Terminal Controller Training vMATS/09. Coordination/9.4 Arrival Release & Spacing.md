# 9.4 Arrival Release & Spacing

## Spacing Agreements

Spacing may be:
- Distance-based (e.g. 5 NM)
- Time-based

---

## ACC Responsibilities

- Deliver aircraft with initial spacing
- Avoid excessive compression

---

## APP Responsibilities

- Adjust spacing using speed and vectoring
- Maintain stable sequence

---

## Common Issue

Poor inbound spacing leads to:
- Increased workload
- Excessive vectoring
- Unstable approaches
