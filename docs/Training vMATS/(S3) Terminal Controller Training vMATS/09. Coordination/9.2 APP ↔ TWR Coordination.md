# 9.2 APP ↔ TWR Coordination

## Arrival Handover

Aircraft shall be transferred to tower when:
- Established on final approach
- Correctly sequenced
- Stable (heading, speed, descent)

---

## Controller Responsibilities (APP)

APP must ensure:
- Correct spacing exists
- No further vectoring required
- Aircraft is on a stable approach

---

## Controller Responsibilities (TWR)

TWR shall:
- Monitor final approach
- Issue landing clearance
- Maintain runway separation

---

## Key Principle

Tower should not need to “fix” the approach.
