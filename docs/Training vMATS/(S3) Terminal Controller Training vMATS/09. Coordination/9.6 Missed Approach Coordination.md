# 9.6 Missed Approach Coordination

## Overview

A missed approach requires immediate coordination between TWR and APP.

---

## Tower Responsibilities

- Issue go-around instruction
- Inform APP immediately

---

## Approach Responsibilities

- Take control of aircraft
- Issue climb instructions
- Reintegrate into sequence

---

## Phraseology

"Go around, fly runway heading, climb to 3000 feet"

---

## Key Principle

Missed approaches must be anticipated and protected at all times.
