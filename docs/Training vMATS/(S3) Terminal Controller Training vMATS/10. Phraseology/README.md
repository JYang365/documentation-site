# Phraseology

Standard phraseology ensures clear and unambiguous communication between controllers and pilots.

---

## General Principles

- Be concise
- Be clear
- Avoid unnecessary words

---

## Radar Instructions

"Turn left heading 180"  
"Climb to 5000 feet"  
"Descend to 3000 feet"

---

## Speed Control

"Reduce speed to 180 knots"  
"Maintain 160 knots to 4 DME"

---

## Approach Clearance

"Cleared ILS approach runway XX"  
"Cleared RNAV approach runway XX"

---

## Go Around

"Go around, fly runway heading, climb to 3000 feet"

---

## Transfer of Communication

"Contact tower 118.1"

---

## Key Principle

> Standard phraseology reduces errors and increases safety.
