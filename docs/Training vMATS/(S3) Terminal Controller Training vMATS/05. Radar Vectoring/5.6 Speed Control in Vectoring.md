# 5.6 Speed Control in Vectoring

Speed control is essential for effective sequencing.

---

## Standard Speeds

- Initial approach: 220 knots
- Base leg: 180 knots
- Final: 160 knots

---

## Application

- Reduce speed to increase spacing
- Maintain higher speed to close gaps

---

## Considerations

- Aircraft performance differences
- Pilot compliance delay
- Phase of flight
