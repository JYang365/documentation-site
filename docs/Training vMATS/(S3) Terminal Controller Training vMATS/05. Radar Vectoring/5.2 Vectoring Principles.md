# 5.2 Vectoring Principles

## Core Principles

- Plan ahead at all times
- Issue clear and timely instructions
- Avoid excessive heading changes
- Maintain situational awareness

---

## Anticipation

Controllers must:
- Predict aircraft paths
- Identify conflicts early
- Adjust sequencing before issues develop

---

## Efficiency

Vectoring should:
- Minimise track miles
- Avoid sharp turns close to final
- Support stable approaches
