
# 5.5 Final Approach Intercept

## Objectives

- Establish aircraft on the localiser
- Ensure correct spacing
- Maintain stable approach profile

---

## Requirements

- Aircraft shall be established before clearance (where applicable)
- Intercept angle should not exceed 30° where possible
- Spacing must be achieved prior to final

---

## Stability

Controllers must ensure:
- Minimal heading corrections on final
- No excessive speed changes
- Smooth transition to tower control
