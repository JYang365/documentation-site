# 7.2 Speed Control Techniques

## Standard Speed Ranges

- ≤ FL100: 250 knots
- Initial approach: 220 knots
- Base leg: 180 knots
- Final: 160 knots

---

## Techniques

### Increasing Spacing
- Reduce speed of leading aircraft
- Maintain speed of trailing aircraft

### Decreasing Spacing
- Increase speed of trailing aircraft
- Maintain higher speeds longer

---

## Timing

Speed instructions must be issued early due to pilot response delay.

---

## Phraseology

"Reduce speed to 180 knots"  
"Maintain 160 knots to 4 DME"
