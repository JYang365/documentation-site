# 7.7 High Workload Management

## Priorities

1. Separation
2. Stability of approaches
3. Efficiency

---

## Techniques

- Simplify instructions
- Avoid unnecessary vectoring
- Build sequence early

---

## Situational Awareness

Controllers must:
- Track all aircraft mentally
- Anticipate conflicts
- Stay ahead of the situation

---

## Warning Signs

- Constant corrections required
- Increasing frequency congestion
- Loss of sequence structure
