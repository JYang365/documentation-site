# 7.1 General

Sequencing is the process of arranging aircraft into an orderly arrival flow for approach and landing.

Speed control is the primary tool used to adjust spacing without excessive vectoring.

---

## Objectives

- Maintain minimum separation
- Deliver stable final approaches
- Minimise controller workload
- Optimise runway throughput

---

## Key Concept

> Good sequencing reduces the need for correction later.
