# 2.3 Circuit Traffic

Circuit traffic operates in close proximity to the aerodrome and requires continuous monitoring by the Tower controller.

---

## 2.3.1 Circuit Positions

Standard circuit positions include:

- Upwind  
- Crosswind  
- Downwind  
- Base  
- Final  

---

## 2.3.2 Managing Circuit Traffic

Tower controllers should:

- Issue clear join instructions  
- Sequence aircraft early  
- Maintain adequate spacing between aircraft  
- Avoid last-minute corrections  

---
