# 2.4 Runway Crossings

Runway crossings require strict control as they involve entering an active runway.

---

## 2.4.1 Before Issuing a Crossing Clearance

Ensure:

- No aircraft is on takeoff roll  
- No aircraft is landing or about to land  
- No aircraft is lined up  
- The crossing can be completed without delay  

---

## 2.4.2 Phraseology

```text
GFA789, cross runway 27, report vacated.
GFA789, hold short runway 27.
