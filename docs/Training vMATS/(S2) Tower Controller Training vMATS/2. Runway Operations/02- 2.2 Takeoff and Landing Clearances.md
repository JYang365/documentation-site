# 2.2 Takeoff and Landing Clearances

Takeoff and landing clearances are the most critical instructions issued by the Tower controller and must only be given when safe.

---

## 2.2.1 Takeoff Clearance

Before issuing takeoff clearance, ensure:

- The aircraft is at the correct runway or intersection  
- The runway is clear  
- Final approach is clear or safe spacing exists  
- Departure release has been obtained (if required)  
- Wake turbulence separation is considered  

### Example

```text
GFA123, wind 270 degrees 8 knots, runway 27, cleared for takeoff.
