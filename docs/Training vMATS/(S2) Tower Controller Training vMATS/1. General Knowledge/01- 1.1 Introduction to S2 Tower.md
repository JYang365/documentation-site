# 1.1 Introduction to S2 Tower

The S2 Tower Controller rating introduces students to aerodrome control operations within the Khaleej VACC.

At this level, controllers are expected to manage aircraft operating on and around the runway, issue takeoff and landing clearances, sequence traffic, maintain situational awareness, and coordinate with surrounding positions.

## Purpose of S2 Training

S2 training prepares students to control a Tower position safely and confidently during normal operations.

Students should develop the ability to:

- Manage runway operations.
- Issue correct Tower instructions and clearances.
- Sequence arriving and departing traffic.
- Handle IFR and VFR aircraft.
- Coordinate with Ground and Approach.
- Respond safely to abnormal situations.

## Expected Standard

An S2 controller should be able to operate a Tower position with limited mentor intervention. The student should show safe judgement, correct phraseology, and good awareness of aircraft operating near the aerodrome.
