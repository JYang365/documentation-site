# 4.3 Special VFR

Special VFR allows a VFR aircraft to operate within controlled airspace when normal VFR conditions are not met, subject to local rules and controller approval.

Special VFR procedures vary between countries and aerodromes. Controllers should always follow Khaleej VACC procedures where published.

## When Special VFR May Be Used

Special VFR may be requested when:

- Visibility is below normal VFR minima.
- Cloud conditions prevent normal VFR operations.
- The aircraft is operating inside controlled airspace.
- The pilot requests Special VFR clearance.

## Tower Considerations

Before approving Special VFR, Tower should consider:

- Current weather conditions.
- IFR traffic operating nearby.
- Runway in use.
- Circuit traffic.
- Local restrictions.
- Whether coordination with Approach is required.
