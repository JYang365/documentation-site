# S2 Tower Controller Training vMATS

The S2 Tower Controller training programme introduces students to aerodrome control operations within the Khaleej vACC.

This course focuses on the safe and efficient management of runway operations, circuit traffic, and coordination with adjacent ATC positions.

---

## Training Sections

The S2 course is divided into the following sections:

1. Aerodrome Control  
2. Responsibilities  
3. Runway Operations  
4. Traffic Management  
5. Coordination  
6. Abnormal and Emergency Situations  

---

## Training Objective

By the end of this course, students should be able to:

- Safely manage runway operations  
- Issue correct clearances and instructions  
- Sequence arriving and departing aircraft  
- Maintain situational awareness  
- Coordinate effectively with Ground and Approach  

---

## Expected Standard

Students are expected to operate a Tower position with minimal supervision, demonstrating safe judgement, correct phraseology, and consistent control techniques.
