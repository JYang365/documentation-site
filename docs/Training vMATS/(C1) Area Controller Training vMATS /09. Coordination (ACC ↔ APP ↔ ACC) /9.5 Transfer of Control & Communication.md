# 9.5 Transfer of Control & Communication

## Transfer of Control

Occurs when responsibility for separation is passed.

---

## Transfer of Communication

Occurs when pilot changes frequency.

---

## Requirements

- Aircraft must be stable
- No unresolved conflicts
- Correct frequency issued

---

## Phraseology

"Contact [unit] on [frequency]"

---

## Key Principle

Transfer must be seamless and require no corrective action.
