# 9.4 ACC ↔ ACC (External) Coordination

## Boundary Coordination

Aircraft crossing FIR boundaries must be:
- At agreed levels
- On correct routing
- Properly spaced

---

## Standard Agreements

- Transfer levels
- Entry/exit points
- Speed restrictions (if applicable)

---

## Controller Responsibilities

- Comply with Letters of Agreement (LOAs)
- Ensure predictable traffic flow

---

## Key Principle

Consistency is critical for cross-boundary operations.
