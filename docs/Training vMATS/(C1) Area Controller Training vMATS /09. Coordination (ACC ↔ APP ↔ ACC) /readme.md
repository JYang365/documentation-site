# Coordination (ACC)

Coordination in an Area Control environment ensures safe and efficient transfer of aircraft between sectors and units.

Effective coordination allows:
- Continuous separation
- Predictable traffic flow
- Reduced controller workload

---

## Objectives

- Ensure seamless transfer between controllers
- Maintain agreed conditions (level, speed, route)
- Prevent conflicts at sector boundaries

---

## Key Principle

> Poor coordination creates conflicts — good coordination prevents them.
