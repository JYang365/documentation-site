# 9.3 ACC ↔ APP Coordination

## Descending Traffic

ACC shall:
- Deliver aircraft at agreed levels
- Ensure appropriate spacing

APP shall:
- Accept aircraft into the arrival sequence
- Provide further descent and vectoring

---

## Climbing Traffic

APP shall:
- Release departures
- Transfer to ACC

ACC shall:
- Integrate departures into en-route traffic

---

## Key Principle

Smooth coordination reduces workload for both units.
