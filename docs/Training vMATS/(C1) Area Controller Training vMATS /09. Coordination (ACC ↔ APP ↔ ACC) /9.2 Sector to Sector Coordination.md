# 9.2 Sector to Sector Coordination

## Internal ACC Transfers

Aircraft shall be transferred between sectors:
- At agreed levels
- On agreed routing
- With no outstanding conflicts

---

## Controller Responsibilities

### Transferring Controller
- Resolve conflicts prior to transfer
- Ensure aircraft is stable

### Receiving Controller
- Accept aircraft in agreed condition
- Integrate into sector traffic

---

## Key Principle

> Do not pass problems to the next sector.
