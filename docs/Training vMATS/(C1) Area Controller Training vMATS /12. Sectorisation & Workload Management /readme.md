# Sectorisation & Workload Management

Sectorisation and workload management are fundamental to safe and efficient area control.

Controllers must manage not only aircraft, but also:
- Airspace complexity
- Traffic demand
- Their own workload

---

## Objectives

- Maintain safe separation
- Prevent controller overload
- Ensure efficient traffic flow
- Enable scalable operations

---

## Key Principle

> A well-managed sector prevents overload before it occurs.
